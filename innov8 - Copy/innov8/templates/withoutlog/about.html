About Us

Welcome to our innovative project, where we harness the power of technology to simulate the most respected role of a teacher in grading student answers and their performance. Our mission is to provide a fair, accurate, unbiased and comprehensive grading solution that not only evaluates the correctness of answers but also takes into account various critical aspects of student responses, including grammar, spelling, formatting, and answer length based on marks and their knowledge level based on Class they are in present .

In today's fast-paced educational environment, teachers face the challenge of providing detailed and consistent feedback, especially when grading large numbers of student submissions. Our project seeks to ease this burden by automating the grading process while maintaining the integrity and thoroughness of human assessment. The system is designed accordingly which helps students improve both their knowledge and communication skills.

Key Features of Our Grading System:
- Correctness: The first step in our grading process is checking the accuracy of the answer in which the AI model Checks weather the answer given is enough to satisfy the question or not. If the answer is incorrect, the score zero is given, else, Score will be given positive but still the scores are confirmed because this doesn’t ensure that a clear standard is maintained .
- Grammar and Spelling: Mistakes in grammar and spelling are crucial indicators of a student's writing ability, and our system meticulously accounts for both. For each spelling mistake, 0.22 points are deducted, and for grammar errors, 0.34 points are subtracted from the final score. This encourages students to submit polished, well-crafted answers.
- Formatting and Answer Length: Proper formatting and an appropriate response length are essential, especially for longer, more complex questions. Our grading system considers these factors in alignment with the difficulty level of the class, ensuring that students provide responses that are not only correct but also well-structured and suitable for the context.
We believe that our approach offers a unique and valuable contribution to modern education by enhancing the way student performance is evaluated, making grading not just faster but also more insightful.
Whether you're an educator, student, or technologist, we invite you to explore our project and see how it can transform the grading experience, fostering both academic growth and deeper learning.
